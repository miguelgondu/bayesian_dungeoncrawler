Adaptive Roguelike applies an approach called Bayesian Optimization to model the time it takes you to solve differently challenging levels. If you want to find out more about the technology behind it, read our paper.

We collect anonymous data about which levels you solve, how long it takes for you to solve them and your playtrace. We do not collect any personal data.

The sprites for this game were taken and adapted from Kenney's.

This work was partially supported by a Google Faculty Research Award 2019.
